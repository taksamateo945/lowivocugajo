Tyler Posey - Bio, Age, Career, Net Worth, Height, Facts

Tyler Posey is an American actor and musician. Tyler Posey was known for his roles as Raul Garcia in Doc, Ty Ventura in Maid in Manhattan, and Scott McCall in the MTV series titled Teen Wolf.

Early Life

Tyler Posey was born in Santa Monica, California, USA, as Tyler Garcia Posey. He is the son of his parents Cyndi Garcia and John Posey who is an actor. They welcomed him into this world on October 18, 1991. Unfortunately, he lost his mother due to breast cancer in December 2014. Furthermore, this actor is of Mexican (mother) and British Isles (father) descent.

He is 32 years old. Moreover, he has three siblings and they are Mayra Posey, Jesse Posey, and Derek Posey. He is generally called by his nicknames Ty and T.Pose. This actor joined Hart High School and used to play the piano in his school days. Currently, he lives in the Los Angeles area with his two dogs.

Career

Tyler developed an early interest in the arts and started his acting career performing on stage with his actor/writer father, at the age of 6. Later, they worked in television and film together. However, he performed in his early years in several children’s film and TV roles. For them, he was twice nominated for a Young Artist Award.

In addition, he was recognized for his roles as Raul Garcia in Doc (2001–2004) and Ty Ventura in Maid in Manhattan (2002). As an adult, this actor is known for playing the central character Scott McCall in the MTV series Teen Wolf (2011–2017). Although he has since been cast in several film roles and has performed in voice acting roles too.

From late 2011 to 2012, he won many youth acting awards, including a Teen Choice Award, and was also nominated for several others. He co-founded the band Lost in Kostko in 2009 and was active for several years. Moreover, he appeared in the film titled Collateral Damage in February 2002 and he played the main character’s son in the romantic comedy titled Maid in Manhattan in December of that year.

In 2007, this musician also voiced a character in the pilot of the Disney original cartoon SheZow. Later, he co-starred in the film White Frog in 2012. Moreover, he was cast in his breakout role as the lead in the MTV television series Teen Wolf, which is based on the 1985 film.

More about his career

Later, in 2015, he became a co-producer for the fifth season of Teen Wolf. On May 24, 2017, this handsome star was cast as Lucas Moreno in the Blumhouse supernatural thriller film titled Truth or Dare. This film was released in theaters on April 13,  2018.

Then, it was announced that he would join the third season of the VH1 slasher TV series Scream on September 25, 2017, and the season premiered on July 8, 2019. Moreover, he would star in Netflix’s animated Fast & Furious TV series titled Fast & Furious: Spy Racers was premiered on December 26, 2019.

Additionally, it was announced that a reunion film for Teen Wolf had been ordered by Paramount+, with Jeff Davis returning as a screenwriter and executive producer of the film. The majority of the original cast members, including him, were set to reprise their roles, with Tyler also serving as a producer. However, this film was released on January 26, 2023.

Relationship Status

Tyler became engaged to his childhood sweetheart Seana Gorlick in the year 2013. Unfortunately, the couple broke off the engagement the same year, after a relationship of 10 years.

Previously, the actor was romantically linked to Bella Thorne, Sophia Ali, and Miley Cyrus. But their relationship did not go well so they separated. Later, he and Phem went public with their relationship in February 2021, and both are dating each other.
