Sam Darnold is an American football quarterback for the New York Jets of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football for the University of Southern California for three-season, starting from 2015.

Sam Darnold Bio; Parents, Education, Height, Weight

Samuel Richard Darnold was born on 5 June 1997 to parents Michael Darnold and Chris Darnold. He grew up in Capistrano Beach, California with older sister Franki Darnold, a college volleyball player at the University of Rhode Island.

Darnold pursued his high school education at San Clemente High School in San Clemente, California. Afterward, he got a scholarship from the University of Southern California to play college football. He stands at a height of 6 feet 3 inches (1.91 meters) and his listed weight is 225 lbs (102 kg).

His Career; College, NFL, Stats

Sam used to play both football and basketball while in high school. He played for the USC Trojans football for three seasons from 2015 to 2017.

Sam Darnold’s NFL career stats (Picture: NFL)

Darnold was selected in the first round, with the third overall selection by the New York Jets of the 2018 NFL Draft.  He made his NFL debut in a preseason game against the Atlanta Falcons in August 2018. Sam made highest completion percentage by a rookie quarterback in a single season in 2018.

Contract & Salary

Sam penned a four-year rookie contract with the Jets, worth $30.25 million in July 2018. It includes a $20,078,338 signing bonus, $30,247,715 guaranteed, and an average annual salary of $7,561,929.
