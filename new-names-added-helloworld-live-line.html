More new names added to the HelloWorld! Live line up

Just when you thought the line up for this year’s HelloWorld! Live couldn’t get any better, eleven amazing new names have been added.

Confirmed to join in with the fun in Birmingham’s Genting Arena on October 28 and 29 are Tom Daley, Em Ford, Saffron Barker, Noodlerella and Roxi. Also joining the crew are Anastasia, Poppy Deyes, Sean Elliott O’Connor and Lana. Additionally, there’ll be performances from Over Atlantic and Leadley.

HelloWorld! Live has been described as “an epic four hour, immersive live show like nothing on Earth” with opportunities to get up close and personal with your favourite YouTubers. Fans will get the chance to walk down the Main Street where they’ll be treated to live music, fun fair rides, games and much more. Later on in the evening, the Emoji House will come alive for an incredible live show and house party.

Want to know more? Check out Jim Chapman’s 7 Things You Need To Know About HelloWorld! video below!

Does HelloWorld! sound like your idea of heaven? Make sure you book your tickets now from SeeTickets or Ticket Factory. VIP tickets are sold out, but worry not! Full day standing and seated stage show tickets are still available.
