Drake Forever Lyrics

Forever is an electrifying anthem that solidified Drake‘s status as a hip-hop powerhouse, propelling him to new heights of success.

Released in 2009 as a collaboration with fellow rap heavyweights Lil Wayne, Kanye West, and Eminem, this dynamic track seamlessly blends each artist’s unique style, resulting in an unforgettable musical masterpiece.
