Debby Ryan Seeks Revenge In New Trailer For Netflix Comedy Series 'Insatiable'

Insatiable: ‘A disgraced civil lawyer-turned-beauty pageant coach takes on a vengeful, bullied teenager as his client.’

Patty (Debby Ryan) has been repeatedly bullied for years from those around her because of her weight. So, when she does something about it and becomes thin and even more beautiful, instead of embracing the attention she is finally getting, Patty has other plans – revenge.

Patty seeks revenge on all who had ever made her feel bad about herself and with the help of Bob Armstrong – a disgraced attorney who’s true love is coaching beauty pageant’s – Patty is coached to become the top pageant queen in the country. However, Bob and his wife, Coralee, didn’t realise how far Patty was willing to go to destroy the lives of others who have wronged her.

Life lesson: Don’t cross Patty, because revenge is too sweet.

Dallas Roberts takes on the role of Bob and Alyssa Milano (Charmed) will take on the role of his wife, Coralee.

Sarah Colonna will be portraying Angie Bladell; ex-lover of alcohol, Weiner Taco’s employee of the month, and most importantly, mother of Patty Bladell.

Erinn V. Westbrook takes the crown as beauty queen champion and the girl everyone wants to be, Magnolia Barnard.

Irene Choi will play Dixie St. Clair – a savage queen who wants the crown more than anyone.

Kimmy Shields plays best friend of Patty and Drew Barrymore enthusiast, Nonnie Thompson.

Michael Provost takes on the role of teen heartthrob, Brick Armstrong.
