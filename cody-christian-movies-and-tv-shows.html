Cody Christian Movies and TV Shows: Comedy Highlights

Cody Christian, born on April 15, 1995, in Portland, Maine, is an American actor known for his comedic roles in movies and TV shows. He rose to fame for his portrayal of Mike Montgomery in the ABC Family series “Pretty Little Liars” and gained further recognition for his role as Theo Raeken in the MTV drama “Teen Wolf.” Cody Christian has also lent his voice to the character Cloud Strife in the video game “Final Fantasy VII Remake.” He has appeared in various live-action films and series, showcasing his comedic talent.
