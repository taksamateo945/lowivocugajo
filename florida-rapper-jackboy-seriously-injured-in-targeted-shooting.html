Shooting of Florida Rapper Jackboy

Details of the Incident

The early morning of December 13, 2023, marked a tragic turn of events for the renowned Florida rapper, Jackboy. The artist, widely recognized for his chart-topping track “GATTI,” fell victim to a deliberate shooting outside his residence in Port St. Lucie’s Traditional neighborhood. Reports indicate that an unidentified assailant approached Jackboy in his driveway, firing multiple shots before swiftly escaping in an unknown vehicle. The Port St. Lucie Police Department (PSLPD) promptly responded to the distressing scene at approximately 6:57 a.m., discovering the rapper with several gunshot wounds. Urgently, Jackboy was airlifted to Lawnwood Hospital in critical condition, and his current health status remains undisclosed.

Response and Investigation

In the wake of this shocking incident, Tradition Preparatory High School, situated just a short distance from the crime scene, implemented a code red lockdown as a precautionary measure. However, the lockdown was lifted at 8:15 a.m., and classes resumed their normal schedule. The PSLPD has taken charge of the investigation, diligently working to uncover the motives behind the targeted attack. They have assured the community that there is no immediate danger, though residents are advised to steer clear of the area due to the heightened police presence.
