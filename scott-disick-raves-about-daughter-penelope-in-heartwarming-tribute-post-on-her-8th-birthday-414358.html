Scott Disick Raves About Daughter Penelope In Heartwarming Tribute Post On Her 8th Birthday!

It’s Penelope Disick’s 8th birthday and her daddy, Scott, did not forget to pay her the sweetest tribute ever on social media! The man took to his platform to post a super cute pic of his only daughter sleeping on his shoulder and in the caption, he wrote a heartfelt message that is bound to bring you to tears so get the tissues ready!

And that was not all! Later on, he shared a second post marking the little girl’s birthday, writing alongside a pic of him holding her by the pool: ‘Holy moly she’s 8!’

https://www.instagram.com/p/CCZXmsQJFfo/

But back to the first post since that one was the most touching!

The loving father wrote earlier today on his IG Stories: ‘Happy birthday to my angel food cake pinop. I could never ever in a million years imagine my life without you! You are my savior and heart [red heart emoji.]’

As fans know, the self-proclaimed lord is also the father to two sons with his ex and Keeping Up with the Kardashians star, Kourtney Kardashian – 5 year old Reign and 10 year old Mason.

He and Kourt were together on and off for almost a decade before splitting back in 2015 but even though they are no longer romantically involved, they have proven time and time again that they are the best at co-parenting their three children.

But since Scott is single again following his split from Sofia Richie back in May, fans have been keeping a very close eye on the exes in case the co-parents reunite!

Either way, both Kourtney and Sofia modeled his Talentless apparel on social media not too long ago so it looks like he’s on good terms with both women.

After all, they could all remain friends and nothing else!
