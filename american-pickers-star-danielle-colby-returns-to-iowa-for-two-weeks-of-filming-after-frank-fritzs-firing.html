American Pickers star Danielle Colby returns to Iowa for two weeks of filming after Frank Fritzs

AMERICAN Pickers star Danielle Colby returned to Iowa for about two weeks of filming after Frank Fritz was fired. 

Danielle, 45, revealed she had “landed in Iowa” in an inspirational and lengthy Instagram post. 

She wrote: “As I calm my bones in a hot bath, preparing for my very full day, I’m thinking on my relationship to this land and the Great Mississippi River.

“I’ve got lots of thoughts about this topic lately.”

The American Pickers star teased and “inside scoop” and “behind-the-scenes photos” from her two-week adventures working on the road with the History show on her Patreon. 

The TV star added: “Have a beautiful Sunday and remember that you can only be where you are.”

PUERTO RICO

The TV personality is currently based in Puerto Rico.

According to the History Channel, Danielle was helping with the rebuilding of the island while she dedicated her time to orphanages and animal shelters.

Though Danielle has been listed as a cast member on the show’s website, she hasn’t appeared on the series as viewers speculated she had left the show.

‘LIKE A BROTHER’

Frank Fritz, 57, also hasn’t appeared on American Pickers in recent seasons due to the grueling back surgery, which left him with about 185 stitches and two rods in his spine. 

Recently, Mike Wolf, 57, confirmed Frank’s exit from the show on social media.

He captioned a photo of the cast: “I have known Frank for as long as I can remember, he’s been like a brother to me.

“The journey that Frank, Dani, and I started back in 2009, like all of life, has come with its highs and lows, blessings and challenges, but it has also been the most rewarding.”

Mike concluded: “I will miss Frank, just like all of you, and I pray for the very best and all good things for him on the next part of his journey.”

‘I WILL MISS FRANK’

Fans have picked sides between Frank and Mike due to their ongoing feud, as viewers have threatened to stop watching American Pickers. 

Frank told the Sun about his side of the feud with Mike.

He said: “I haven’t talked to Mike in two years. He knew my back was messed up, but he didn’t call me up and ask how I was doing. That’s just how it is.

Frank added: “That’s fine. It’s like you’ve got Aerosmith and there’s Steven Tyler and he’s the front man. I found my spot, I’m second and he’s number one on the show. 

“That’s no problem with me, maybe he does have a problem.”
