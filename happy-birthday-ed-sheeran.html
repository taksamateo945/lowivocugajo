Happy birthday, Ed Sheeran! - CelebMix

Ed Sheeran turns 25 today and here at CelebMix, we love celebrating our favourite celeb birthdays. You may have noticed a peak in coverage of the British singer on our website, starting with an article on why Ed Sheeran would be your perfect Valentine.

But we didn’t stop there. For your reading, viewing and hearing pleasure (okay, and for our own), we compiled lists of Ed’s best covers, best music videos, best collabs, his best lyrics, our favorite instagram posts and his top 5 tattoos. And there’s even more to come in the next few days!

Teddy may be on a social media hiatus right now, but that won’t keep us from celebrating. We’ll get ourselves some Nandos tonight and pray for Mr. Sheeran to break his hiatus to share a festive snapchat or something of the like.

We suggest you join us in celebrating what just might be the most talented ginger Britain has ever produced (sorry Eddie Redmayne, we still love you). Grab a drink, put Jumpers for Goalposts on and sing your Ed Sheeran-loving heart out.

Ed’s hiatus is expected to last at least until fall, and we hope that he’ll come back re-energised and with a bunch of new music to share with us. We’ll be right here waiting.

Happy 25th birthday Ed!
