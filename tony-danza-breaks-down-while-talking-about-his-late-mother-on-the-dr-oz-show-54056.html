33 Years LaterSee Tony Danza, Judith Light, and the Cast of Whos the Boss? Then and Now!

Back in 1984, Who’s the Boss? surfaced TV screens everywhere — and made all its dedicated viewers laugh until 1992!

On this day 25 years ago, the last episode of the iconic sitcom sadly aired — and we’ve missed the show’s funny storyline ever since! That’s why today, we’re taking a look back at Tony Danza, Judith Light, and the rest of the lovable cast then and now.

In a previous interview back in September 2015, Alyssa Milano revealed she’d love to reprise her role as Samantha Micelli if the show were to be brought back to TV after all this time!
