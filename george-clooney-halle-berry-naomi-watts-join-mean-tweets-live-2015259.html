George Clooney, Halle Berry, Naomi Watts Join Mean Tweets Live

Halle Berry‘s not afraid of her imperfections! The Extant star was among the latest gang of celebrity “Mean Tweets” readers to join Jimmy Kimmel Live! on Thurdsay, Spet. 24 — and the subject of her unkind social media message was her breasts.

Leaving little to the imagination, Berry looked stunning in a see-through black turtleneck, revealing a black lace bra (which we recognize from her August appearance on the show!) as she stood in front of the audience to read out her tweet.

“Halle Barrie’s boobs are lopsided,” @ashton_ wrote and Berry smirked.

“Well, when they’re real, that happens!” the gorgeous 49-year-old replied, before leaving the stage triumphantly.

Tobey Maguire, Naomi Watts, and George Clooney were also on hand to help Kimmel show his viewers that celebs have feelings too.
